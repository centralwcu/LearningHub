Transferring to an External Account

This guide will help you move money from your account to another bank or credit union (called an 'external account'). You'll learn how to set up the connection and send money safely.

1
Click "Move Money"
2
Click "External Transfers & Payments"
3
Click "Make a Transfer"
4
Enter the transfer amount
5
Choose the account to transfer from
6
Select the desired account
7
Choose the account to transfer to
8
Click "Add External Account"
9
Search for the bank and select it
10
Log in to your other bank to verify your account.
11
If your bank isn't listed, click Enter account details.
12
Enter the account holder's name
13
Enter the Routing and Account numbers
14
Select the account type
15
Click Continue
16
If you enter your other account's details manually, we'll send a small amount to that account. Check your other bank in 1-3 days, find the deposit, and use the code to finish setting up your transfer.
17
The code will appear inside the transaction details. It will be a four- or three-digit code and will display differently depending on the Financial Institution.
Once received, return to the Activity tab in External Transfers & Payments to enter the code. If you do not see a yellow banner requesting the code, follow the below steps.
18
Go to the "External Transfers & Payments" page, and click the three vertical dots on the "Activity" tab
19
Click "Settings"
20
Click "Transfer"
21
Click "Manage Accounts"
22
Find the pending External Account at the bottom and click "Verify"
23
The first screen will show an example code. Click "Next".
24
Fill in the code provided then click "Continue".
