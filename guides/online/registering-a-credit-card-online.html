Registering a credit card online

This guide will help you register your credit card for online access. Once registered, you can view your balance, make payments, and manage your card online.

1
Click "Credit Card"
2
Review the terms and conditions and then check this box
3
Click "NEXT"
4
Enter in the credit card number, the last 4-digits of the Primary Cardholder's Social Security Number, date of birth and the 5 digit zip code
If your card isn't accepted, double-check the card number and your personal information.
5
Click "NEXT"
6
Enter your phone number and email address then click "NEXT".
7
Select three security questions and provide answers
8
Examples of the security questions available
9
Click "SUBMIT"
10
Click "OK" and you will be taken to your Credit Card account page
You can also use the CW Card Controls app to manage your card from your smartphone. Download it from the Google Play or Apple app store.
