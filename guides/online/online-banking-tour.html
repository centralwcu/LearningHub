Online Banking tour

This guide offers a comprehensive overview of CWCU's online banking features. Overall, this guide is essential for anyone looking to maximize the benefits of CWCU's banking services.

Mobile Banking: You can access your accounts on the go with our CWCU Mobile Banking app, available for iOS and Android. Most features in this guide are also available in the app.
1
The "Accounts" page lists all accessible accounts. Business and personal accounts are separated by profiles-use the profile switcher (top right) to change profiles. Your member number is shown here (1) and is needed for password resets or unlocking your account.
2
You can adjust the size of the account tiles
3
Click an account to view more details, such as transaction history.
4
View your transaction history, account details, and ACH number here. Use the ACH number for direct deposits or electronic debits. Click the eye to unmask your ACH number.
5
Click the three dots next to a transaction to add a note, ask about the transaction, or download/print it.
6
Clicking the Central Willamette logo will return to your landing page.
7
You can also open a new account from the main screen.
8
The "Related Links" window provides quick access to popular features.
9
Use the "Quick Transfer" window to transfer funds directly from the Accounts page.
10
"Move Money" is where you transfer between accounts, use Bill Pay, or send money to non-CWCU members.
11
"External Transfers & Payments" is the place to pay bills, send money to other people externally, and transfer to accounts at other financial institutions.
12
"Activity" shows your history of bill payments, person-to-person payments, and external transfers.
13
"Pay Bills" is where you add and pay billers. You can also schedule bills to pay on a recurring basis.
14
"Pay a Person" lets you send money to anyone, even if they aren't a CWCU member. You just need their email address or cell phone number.
15
"Transfer" sends money from your CWCU account to an external account at another financial institution.
16
"Internal Transfers & Payments" is for moving money between your CWCU accounts and for paying your loans.
17
"Member-2-Member" allows you to transfer to other Central Willamette accounts that you are not a signer on, and between your business and personal accounts.
18
"Payment by Debit/Credit Card" allows you to pay loans with any debit or credit card.
19
Toggle this option to make the Move Money page your default landing page after login.
20
"Credit Card" displays your CWCU credit card info, transaction history, and payment options.
The Credit Card page will open in a new tab
21
Click "More" to access additional online banking options.
22
"Alerts" lets you manage account alerts (balance, deposit, withdrawal) and choose how you receive them.
23
"Security" is where you change your User ID, password, manage One-Time Passcodes, and registered devices.
24
Under "Apply Online" you can apply for loans or open new CWCU accounts online.
25
"Settings" lets you update contact info and manage how your accounts appear in online banking.
26
"Utilities" is where you access eStatements, set travel notifications, complete forms, and more.
27
"Inbox" shows account notifications and lets you view or send messages to CWCU support.
28
The first page of "Inbox" will show security alerts and account notifications.
29
The Support Messages page lets you send or view messages with Support.
30
Use the "Navigate to" field to quickly go to any page in Online Banking.
31
Type a keyword (like 'transfer') to see all related pages.
32
Troubleshooting: If you see an error message or something doesn't look right, try refreshing your browser, trying a different browser or logging out and back in. For help, click "Inbox" to send a message to Support, or visit our Help Center.
Security Tips:
  • Never share your password or security codes.
  • Always log out when you're done, especially on shared devices.
  • Watch out for emails or texts asking for your login info-these could be phishing scams.
