Send Money to a Person

This feature can be used to send money to anyone, instantly. All you need is their email or phone number.

ⓘ
When you use Pay A Person, the recipient will receive a text or email with steps to receive the funds. The funds can be sent to their: - Account at Central Willamette (Instant transfer) - Account at another financial institution (1-2 business days) - Debit card (Instant transfer) (70 cent surcharge)
1
Once logged in, click "Move Money"
2
Click "External Transfers & Payments"
3
Click "Pay A Person"
4
Click "Add Contact"
5
Enter in the recipient's First Name, Last Name, Mobile Number and/or Email.
Double-check their email and phone number for accuracy!
6
Choose how to deliver the payment (email or text).
7
Select the default delivery method.
ⓘ
The recipient will receive a text or email with steps to claim the funds based on the default delivery method selected.
8
Set a Security Question and Answer for the recipient to claim the funds.
ⓘ
Consider choosing a Security Question that the recipient can answer. Otherwise, remember to provide the recipient with the answer.
9
Click Save and Pay to send funds now, or Save to add the recipient for later.
10
The person is now saved under Contacts.
ⓘ
Clicking the pencil icon next to a contact will allow you to edit that contact.
11
Select the contact and click Next to send funds.
12
Enter the amount, select the source account, and set the payment frequency. Click Pay.
13
These are the options presented to the recipient to receive their funds.
14
Central Willamette Direct: Full Member Number (with the leading zeros), Share number and Account type.
15
Debit Card: Full Card Number, Cardholder's Name and Expiration date.
16
Bank Account: Routing and Account Number, Account Type and the Account Category (Checking, Savings, etc)
Only send money to people you know and trust. To learn more about Financial Scams, click here: <https://www.centralwcu.org/resources/personal-resources/financial-scams-to-avoid-and-how-to-protect-yourself/>
