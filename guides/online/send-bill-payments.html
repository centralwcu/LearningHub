Send Bill Payments

This guide will help you send Bill Payments to saved billers.

1
Click "Move Money"
2
Click "External Transfers & Payments"
3
Click "Pay Bills"
4
Find the biller you want to pay
5
Choose the account to pay from.
6
(Optional) Add a memo to the payment.
7
Click the Deliver Date to select when funds will be sent and set payment frequency.
The "Repeats" dropdown lets you pick if the payment should get sent out weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, etc.
8
(Optional) Enable expedited payment for a fee.
9
The delivery date will update if you expedite.
Electronic payments usually arrive in a few business days without expediting.
10
Click the orange PAY button to schedule and send your payment.
