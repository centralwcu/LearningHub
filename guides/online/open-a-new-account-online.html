Open a New Account Online

This guide is designed to walk you through the process of opening a new account when logged into Online Banking

1
Select "Open a New Account' from the Accounts page.
2
Most of your information will automatically be filled in, but review for accuracy then scroll down.
3
Fill in the information at the bottom then click "eConsent" to review the agreement.
4
Close the document after reviewing.
5
Check the box to agree to eConsent terms.
6
Click "Save and Continue".
7
Enter your address information if it is not pre-filled in.
8
Confirm if the Mailing Address is the same. Click "Continue".
9
Select your employment status and income source.
10
Indicate if you want to add a co-applicant.
11
Click "Save and Continue".
12
Choose the accounts you want to open. Membership Savings is required
13
Select any optional services you want.
14
There are additional account options
15
Click "Save and Continue"
16
Choose if you want to add beneficiaries.
17
Click "Save and Continue"
18
Indicate if you are subject to backup withholding.
19
Create a passphrase and select how you heard about CWCU.
20
Click "Save and Continue"
21
Upload a photo of your government-issued ID, and Address Verification if it is different than what is on your ID
22
Drag and drop or select files from your device to upload.
23
Once uploaded, your file will appear below.
24
Click "Submit"
25
Click "Save and Continue"
26
Review your information.
27
Review and accept the disclosures, then click "Submit my application" to finish.
Your application will be reviewed. Next steps, such as funding your account, will be provided after.
