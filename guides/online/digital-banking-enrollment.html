Digital Banking Enrollment

This guide will show you how to enroll into Digital Banking for the first time on a web browser.

1
Navigate to [centralwcu.org](https://centralwcu.org) and click "Online Banking Login"
2
Click on "Enroll in online banking"
3
Review the Electronic Services Agreement, click the box to consent then click "Next"
4
Enter in your date of birth, social security number and either your debit/credit card number, member number or mailing zip code. Then click "Next".
5
Enter in your desired User ID and Password then click "Next". Be sure to check the requirements on the right if your desired User ID or Password are not working.
6
The phone numbers on your account will auto-generate in the list. Select which one you want to receive a one-time passcode to and how you want to receive it. Then click "Next"
7
Enter in the One-Time Passcode then click "Next"
8
You are now enrolled! Click on "Log in to digital banking" to login with your new User ID and password.
