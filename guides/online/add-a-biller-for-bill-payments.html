Add a Biller for Bill Payments

This guide will help you add a biller for Bill Payments so you can easily send payments online.

1
Click "Move Money"
2
Click "External Transfers & Payments"
3
Click "Pay Bills"
4
Click "Add Biller"
5
Search for and select your biller
6
Log in with your biller credentials, or choose to add the biller manually.
7
Enter your Account Number for the biller
If you're not sure of your account number, check your recent bill or contact the company you want to pay.
8
Enter the biller's ZIP code (found on your bill statement).
9
If you don't know your account number, then select this option
Payments to billers without an account number are sent by paper check and may take longer to arrive.
10
Enter the biller's address (from your statement).
11
Click "Next"
12
The biller will now appear on the Pay Bills page.
