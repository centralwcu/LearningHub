Delete Bill Pay Billers

This guide will show you how to remove a biller from your list of Bill Pay payees

1
Click "Move Money"
2
Click "External Transfers & Payments"
3
Tap "Pay Bills"
4
Click on the biller to delete
5
Click the trash can icon
6
Click "Confirm"
If you have trouble editing or deleting a payment or biller, please contact our support team for help at the below number in the footer, or by submitting a ticket on the 'Support' page.
