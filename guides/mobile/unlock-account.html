Unlock Account

This guide is designed to help you get back into your account if you are locked out.

1
Tap "Need help logging in?"
2
Tap "Unlock Account"
3
Enter your Member Number and User ID, tap "Next"
4
Enter your Social Security Number, Date of Birth then tap "Next"
5
Tap "Text me"
6
Enter in the One-Time Passcode, tap "Submit", then select if you want to reset your password.
You will receive an email/text message when the account is unlocked.
