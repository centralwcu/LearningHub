Send a Bill Payment

Bill Pay lets you pay your bills (like utilities, credit cards, or phone service) directly from your bank account using your phone. This guide will show you how to do it step by step.

1
Tap "Move Money"
2
Tap "External Transfers and Payments"
3
Tap "Pay Bills"
The Activity page shows your scheduled and past payments.
4
Tap the [[+]] to add a biller. Saved billers appear at the bottom.
5
Search for your biller.
If not found, select Add Manually and continue to step 7.
6
Log in to your biller account, or tap "Add Biller Manually" if you don't have login info.
7
Enter your biller account number and zip code.
Find your account number and biller zip code on your bill or statement. For credit cards, use the 16-digit card number.
8
If you don't have an account number, select "I don't have account number" and enter the biller's address. Payments will be mailed as a check.
9
The new biller will appear on the Pay Bills page.
10
Tap the amount to edit it. Tap the arrow to set the delivery date and payment frequency.
11
Tap the date to open the calendar and select a send/delivery date. You can expedite payments for a fee (not available for electronic payments).
Electronic payments cannot be Expedited since they arrive within a couple business days.
