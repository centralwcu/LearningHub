Open a New Account in the Mobile App

This guide walks you through how to open a new account with Central Willamette Credit Union using the mobile app.

1
Tap "Apply for new account"
2
Select the membership for the new account
3
Fill in the required information. Review the eConsent terms, mark the checkbox then tap "Save and Continue"
If you can't continue, check that all required fields are filled in.
4
Review the address, then tap "Save and Continue"
5
Enter in Employment History and add any additional Co-Applicants, then tap "Save and Continue"
6
Select the accounts to open, then tap "Save and Continue"
7
Select if you are subject to backup withholding, then tap "Save and continue"
8
Review the information and agree to the "General Inquiry Disclosure"
9
Check the box agreeing to the "Agreements and Disclosures", then select "Submit my application".
Someone will reach out to you with next steps after application review.
