Access eStatements

eStatements are electronic versions of your statement. This guide is designed to show you how to sign up and view your eStatements on the CW Mobile Banking app.

1
Tap here to bring up the menu
2
Tap on "eStatements"
3
Tap on "View Statements"
A new tab will open in your phone's browser
If this is the first time accessing eStatements, then you will need to review and agree to the terms and conditions.
4
Tap "Register"
5
Fill in your name, email address then tap "next"
6
Review the disclosures, check the box to agree then tap "Finish"
7
Use the menu (three lines) to manage eStatement settings or update your email.
8
Change document type (e.g., 1099s, loan coupons) or date range as needed
9
Tap a document name to view, print, or download it
10
The status row shows if a document has been viewed
