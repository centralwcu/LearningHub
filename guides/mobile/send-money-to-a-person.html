Send Money to a Person

This feature can be used to send money to anyone, instantly. All you need is their email or phone number.

When you use Pay A Person, the recipient will receive a text or email with steps to receive the funds. The options available to the recipient are to send the funds to their: - Account at Central Willamette (Instant transfer) - Account at another financial institution (1-2 business days) - Debit card (Instant transfer) (70 cent surcharge)
1
Tap "Move Money"
2
Tap "External Transfers and Payments"
3
Tap "Pay A Person"
All scheduled payments and transfers display on the Activity page
4
Added people appear at the bottom. Tap the pencil to edit, or the + to add a new payee.
5
Enter the person’s info and choose how to deliver the payment (email or text). Set a Security Question—they must answer it to claim funds.
Double-check their email and phone number for accuracy!
Future payments will use saved info, but the Security Question is always required.
6
Select the person to pay and tap "Next"
Clicking the pencil icon next to a contact will allow you to edit that contact.
7
Enter the amount, optional memo, select the account, and set the date/frequency. Tap "Pay".
The recipient will receive a text or email with steps to claim the funds based on the default delivery method selected.
8
These are the options presented to the recipient to receive their funds.
9
Central Willamette Direct: Full Member Number (with the leading zeros), Share number and Account type.
10
Debit Card: Full Card Number, Cardholder's Name and Expiration date.
11
Bank Account: Routing and Account Number, Account Type and the Account Category (Checking, Savings, etc)
