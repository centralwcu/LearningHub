How to delete saved people for external payments on the Mobile App

This guide walks you through the steps for: How to delete saved people for external payments on the Mobile App.

1
Tap "Move Money"
2
Tap "External Transfers & Payments"
3
To delete a saved person, tap "Pay a Person" then tap the pencil next to the person
4
Tap the garbage can icon
5
Tap on "Delete"
