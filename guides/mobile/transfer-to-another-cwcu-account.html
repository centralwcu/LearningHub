Transfer to Another CWCU Account

This guide will show you how to send money to another Central Willamette Credit Union (CWCU) account using our mobile app. You can send money to your own accounts or to someone else's account at CWCU.

To transfer between Business and Personal accounts, add each as an internal recipient.
1
Tap "Move Money"
2
Tap "Member-2-Member Setup"
3
Tap Personal Recipient (or Business Recipient if using a business account).
4
To add a new recipient, tap Add Recipient. - Use the three dots to edit or delete existing recipients.
5
Enter a name for the recipient and tap "Next"
6
Tap "Add Account" under Account details.
7
Enter the recipient's Member Number, Last Name/Company Name, and a nickname. - Select the account type for the deposit.
For business accounts, enter the exact business name as it appears on the business's account page.
8
Confirm the information and tap "Save"
9
A confirmation will appear when the recipient is added.
10
The new recipient will show under Transfer Funds on the Move Money page.
11
Tap a recipient to go directly to the transfer screen; the recipient will be auto-filled in the "To" field.
ⓘ
Recipients will also appear as 'To' options on the Member-2-Member page.
