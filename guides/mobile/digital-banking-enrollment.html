Digital Banking Enrollment

This guide will show you how to enroll into Digital Banking for the first time on our Mobile App.

1
Open up the Mobile App and tap "New user? Register now"
2
Review the Electronic Services Agreement, check the box then tap "Next"
3
Fill in your date of birth, social security number and either your debit/credit card number, member number or mailing zip code then tap "Next"
4
Enter in your desired User ID and password based on the requirements listed then tap "Next"
5
Select your preferred contact preferences then tap "Next"
6
Select how you would like to receive your one-time passcode
7
Enter in the code you received and tap "Submit"
8
Tap "Next" after reviewing your information
9
The next screen will show you the accounts that you are on and your ownership type. You can tap the little carrot to see the individual accounts. Tap "Next" after reviewing.
10
Select your default accounts for Text Banking, Mobile Deposits or Funds Transfers. You can also tap "Skip" without selecting any accounts. Tap "Save" if you did select any default accounts.
You are now enrolled in Digital Banking and will be prompted with the option to login with your new User ID and password.
