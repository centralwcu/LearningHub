Cancel or Edit Scheduled Internal Transfers

This guide will show you how to cancel or edit scheduled transfers.

1
Tap "Move Money"
2
Tap on "View Scheduled Transfers"
3
Tap "Within CU"
4
Tap under "Accounts" then select the account that has the transfer to cancel
5
Tap "Delete" to delete the transfer or tap "edit" if you need to change the transfer
6
Tap "OK" to confirm you want to cancel the scheduled transfer
