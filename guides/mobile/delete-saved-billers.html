Delete Saved Billers

This guide will show you how to remove companies or people you pay regularly (called "billers") using our mobile app.

1
Tap "Move Money"
2
Tap "External Transfers & Payments"
3
Tap on "Pay Bills" and then tap on the Biller to Delete
4
Tap on the garbage can icon
5
Tap "Confirm"
