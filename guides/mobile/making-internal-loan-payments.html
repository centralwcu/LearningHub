Making Internal Loan Payments

This guide demonstrates how to complete payments to internal CWCU loans on the Mobile App.

1
Tap "Move Money"
2
Tap "Internal Transfers and Payments"
3
Tap "Select a source account" to select the account the payment will come out of
4
Select an account
5
Tap "Select destination account" to choose the loan you want to pay
6
When a loan is selected as the destination account, then the "Payment option" will appear and will let you select to make a regular payment, pay the next due amount, etc.
7
When you select "Make a regular payment" then you can enter an amount to pay in the "Enter amount" field.
ⓘ
The transfer date for the payment will default to today's date, but you can change the date to schedule a future payment and checking the box next to "Repeat transfer" will let you schedule recurring payments
8
After hitting "Transfer funds" you will be brought to the confirm screen. Review the information and hit "Confirm transfer"
9
Tap "Done"
