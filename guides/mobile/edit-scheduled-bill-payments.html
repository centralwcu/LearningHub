Edit Scheduled Bill Payments

This guide will show you how to change or remove bill payments you have set up in advance using our mobile app.

1
Tap "Move Money"
2
Tap "External Transfers & Payments"
3
Tap the scheduled Bill Payment to delete or edit
4
Tap "Delete" if you want to remove the payment, or "Edit" if you want to change the amount or date.
